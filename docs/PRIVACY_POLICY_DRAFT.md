## 1. Overview

ReadingDeck is a service that helps users search, save, and revisit book-based reading cards. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, where it is stored, and what choices users have.

If you use the ReadingDeck ChatGPT app, this policy applies to your use of that app and the related ReadingDeck services.

## 2. Information We Collect

We may collect the following categories of information:

### A. Account and authentication information

- Basic account identifiers required to sign you in to ReadingDeck
- OAuth-related identifiers and tokens used to connect the ChatGPT app to your ReadingDeck account
- Provider information such as the login method you used

### B. User content

- Books you add to your ReadingDeck library
- Cards you create, including:
  - card type
  - title
  - quote
  - thought
  - page information
- Search queries and requests you submit through the ChatGPT app

### C. Technical and usage information

- Request logs needed for security, reliability, and debugging
- Error logs and tool invocation logs
- Basic device, browser, and network metadata made available by our hosting providers

### D. Book metadata and cover images

- Book title, author, publisher, and descriptive metadata
- Cover image URLs or uploaded cover image references

## 3. How We Use Information

We use collected information to:

- authenticate you and maintain your session
- display your saved books and cards
- search your existing reading cards
- create new books and cards at your request
- fetch book search results from third-party book search providers
- improve reliability, debug failures, and prevent abuse

We do not use your ReadingDeck library content to advertise to you.
